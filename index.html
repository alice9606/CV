Hi! I am Yukun, a 24 year-old girl from China.

In the past six years, I have visited Shanghai, Lund and Amsterdam, and have studied advertising and media and communication, in the fields of marketing, social and behavioral sciences.

Inspired by my Master's thesis and personal life, my current research interest is gamification and game studies, user and player experiences, digital health and well-being.
